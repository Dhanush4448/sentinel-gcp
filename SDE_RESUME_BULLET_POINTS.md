## Formatting Tips for Resume

1. **Keep it to 4 bullets max** - Recruiters scan quickly
2. **Start with action verbs** - "Architected", "Built", "Designed", "Implemented"
3. **Include technologies** - Next.js, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, GCP
4. **Show impact** - Production deployment, scalability, security compliance
5. **Be specific** - "4-tier RBAC" not "role-based access", "0-10 instances" not "auto-scaling"
